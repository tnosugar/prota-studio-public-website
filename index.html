Two surfaces, one framework.

Products

Demand for an AI-era product. Which of N concepts to fund. Whether a feature, pricing model, or platform direction has commercial signal. Whether to commit, reshape, or kill before the next capital call.

Communications

Which growth strategy, channel mix, or positioning will move the KPIs. Which message lands with which segment. Whether a brand expression compounds or merely costs.

Both treated as commercial questions. Evidence collected from real customers (or the closest analog), instrumented, and delivered as a decision-ready memo, not a deck.

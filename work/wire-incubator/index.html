Curriculum partnership · Web3 / blockchain · Multi-cohort

Our methodology, taught end-to-end as a public curriculum.

A twelve-week Web3 startup curriculum operated as a Prota Ventures partnership with Wire Network. Validation methodology adapted for community-classroom delivery, with $100K demo-day checks and Wire Grants for top teams.

12-week curriculum partnership (Mar-May 2026) Web3 / blockchain Multi-cohort, ~15 candidate projects Curriculum design, weekly hosting, validation methodology adaptation, demo-day orchestration

Challenge

Wire Network, a Layer-1 blockchain protocol, wanted a public, community-based startup program that would shape Web3 builders from idea to demo-day in twelve weeks. The methodology had to be rigorous enough to produce fundable teams, accessible enough for builders new to Web3, and scaffolded with enough structure to run weekly across a multi-cohort audience. The validation question for us was operational: does our methodology hold up when adapted from single-client engagements to a community-classroom format?

Solution

A twelve-week curriculum delivered through weekly live sessions on Discord, recorded and archived to a Notion home with downloadable tools and templates. Prota Ventures hosts; Wire Network provides BD, marketing, and community infrastructure; external experts cover the technically-deep modules.

The curriculum walks teams across the full lifecycle of a Web3 startup:

  1. Program introduction and team structure.
  2. Idea refinement, assumptions, audience, persona, user journey, customer interviews, demand testing, product one-pager, competitive matrix. The validation core; closest mapping to our Learning Sprint methodology.
  3. The Web3 startup, taxonomy, decentralization levers, Web3 assumptions checklist, "why Web3?" gut-check.
  4. How to incorporate, Day One Law as external expert.
  5. Tokenization, Denarii Labs as external expert.
  6. Building in public.
  7. Building modern products, AMA with Wire / Prota leadership on validation-as-discipline-in-the-AI-era.
  8. Go-to-market.
  9. Fundraising and pitching.
  10. Demo Day, vetted applicant register, $100K checks for top teams, Wire Grants.

Results

  • Curriculum-shaped delivery proven. Our methodology held up across a community-classroom format with external co-host, external experts, and a multi-cohort audience.
  • Web3 Assumptions Checklist authored as a public artifact, now a candidate skill-bundle inside Prota's methodology library.
  • Decentralization Levers Scorecard, a six-axis framework (Governance, IP, Revenue, Ownership, Membership, Identity) portable beyond Web3 and now a topic-map entry in our methodology library.
  • Vetted demo-day applicant register, the clearest picture of who the program drew and what they were building.
  • Public articulation of validation-as-discipline-in-the-AI-era, a sharpening of the Prota Studios brand thesis that informs current positioning.

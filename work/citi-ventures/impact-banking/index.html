ESG-aligned banking, sharpened to the right surface.

Challenge.

Citi had a values-led investing thesis surfaced two years earlier in Impact-Investful (2018) that had not been carried forward into a shipped product. The decision in 2020 was: same strategic thesis, sharper surface? An ESG-aligned bank account with deposits and a debit card felt more concrete than an ESG-aligned investing app.

Approach.

  • Demand test under a fresh unbranded domain with the digital-bank value proposition.
  • Three product pillars on test: ESG-aligned deposits, ethical-footprint tracker, and recycled-plastic debit card.
  • Run as a parallel track within Workplace Banking, sharing audience and channel infrastructure.

Outcome.

  • Validated as a track, demand signal sufficient to carry the values-led-banking direction forward.
  • Ethical-footprint tracker emerged as the strongest of the three product pillars.

Methodology lessons.

  • Same strategic thesis, sharper surface is a valid product-shape iteration when the original test surfaced demand for the underlying value but not for the proposed shape.

We do one thing well.

Prota Studios is a validation studio. We have run 100-plus product experiments at Citi Ventures, demand tests for Visa, Bird Buddy, and Stix, and innovation validation for Clorox, Daikin, Exelon, and Audubon. Across nine years and a wide spread of industries, the work has stayed the same shape: turn the riskiest commercial assumptions into evidence, on a clock the business actually has.

Why focus on validation.

AI has collapsed the cost of building. The teams we work with are shipping faster than at any point in the last decade, and they are increasingly unsure whether what they ship will land. The bottleneck used to be build. Now it is knowing.

We chose to specialize. Other firms, including our sister entity Prota Ventures, handle incubation, design, build, and growth. Prota Studios runs validation. One methodology, two cadences (episodic Learning Sprints and ongoing Continuous Validation programs), pointed at the one question every team is now wrestling with: does this work?

How we run.

Multi-disciplinary, full team

Every engagement is run by a multi-disciplinary Prota team, researchers, designers, analysts, instrumented test engineers. Sized to the scope. We commit to a full team, not a single consultant. That is what makes a thorough validation program deliverable in weeks instead of quarters.

Agentic inside the delivery

We use the same agentic tools our clients are shipping with, applied to research synthesis, test build, instrumentation, and qualitative coding. The evidence standard does not change. The timeline compresses.

Decision-ready output

Every engagement ends in a two-page memo. Every load-bearing assumption is marked supported, invalidated, or inconclusive, with evidence attached and a confidence level named. Recommendation is explicit: kill, reshape, or commit. Built for a board to read in five minutes.

The Prota Ventures ecosystem.

Prota Studios is a sister entity to Prota Ventures, sharing a single mission: promoting human flourishing through entrepreneurship. Prota Ventures is the startup incubator and venture capital arm, they incubate new businesses, deploy capital, and run the broader founder ecosystem. Prota Studios is the validation services practice.

The relationship is reciprocal. Studio engagements compound inside the ecosystem: validated bets move into the Ventures pipeline; Ventures portfolio companies get validation work as needed. When a client's question shifts beyond validation, they need to incubate, design, build, or grow, we route to ecosystem partners. We are the validation specialist; the ecosystem covers the rest.
